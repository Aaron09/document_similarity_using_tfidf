sevenlayer systemedit




seven layers forest garden


robert hart pioneered system based observation natural forest divided distinct levels used intercropping develop existing small orchard apples pears edible polyculture landscape consisting following layers

‘canopy layer’ consisting original mature fruit trees
‘lowtree layer’ smaller fruit trees dwarfing root stocks
‘shrub layer’ fruit bushes currants berries
‘herbaceous layer’ perennial vegetables herbs
‘rhizosphere’ ‘underground’ dimension plants grown roots tubers
‘ground cover layer’ edible plants spread horizontally
‘vertical layer’ vines climbers

component sevenlayer system plants selected traditional vegetable crops grown today carrots loving plants well selected shady forest garden system hart favoured shade tolerant perennial vegetables
