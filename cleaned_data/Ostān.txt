modern historyedit
iran held modern territory since second half 19th century treaty paris 1857 1906 1950 iran divided twelve provinces ardalan azerbaijan baluchestan fars gilan araqe ajam khorasan khuzestan kerman larestan lorestan mazandaran2
1950 iran reorganized form numbered provinces subordinate governorates gilan mazandaran east azerbaijan west azerbaijan kermanshah khuzestan fars kerman khorasan isfahan2
