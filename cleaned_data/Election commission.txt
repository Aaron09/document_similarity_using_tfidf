independent modeledit
independent model election commission independent executive manages budget countries independent election commission include australia canada india indonesia jordan nigeria pakistan poland romania south africa lanka thailand united kingdom countries independence election commission constitutionally guaranteed section constitution south africa
branch modeledit
branch model election commission often called electoral branch usually constitutionallyrecognized separate branch government members appointed either executive legislative branch countries electoral branch include bolivia costa rica panama nicaragua venezuela
mixed modeledit
mixedmodel independent board determine policy implementation usually matter executive department varying degrees supervision independent board countries model include cameroon france germany japan senegal spain
executive modeledit
executive model election commission directed cabinet minister part executive branch government include local government authorities acting agents central body countries model include denmark singapore sweden switzerland tunisia united states elections federal state local offices executive branch state government example division elections florida department state1
judicial modeledit
judicial model election commission closely supervised ultimately responsible special electoral court countries model include argentina brazil mexico
