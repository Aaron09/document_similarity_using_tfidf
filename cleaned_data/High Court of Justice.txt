originally high court consisted five divisions—the king’s bench common pleas exchequer chancery probate divorce admiralty divisions 1880 common pleas exchequer divisions abolished leaving three divisions probate divorce admiralty division renamed family division administration justice 1970 jurisdiction reorganised accordingly
