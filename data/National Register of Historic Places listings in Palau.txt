



Location of Palau in the western Pacific


This is a list of the buildings, sites, districts, and objects listed on the National Register of Historic Places in the Republic of Palau. There are currently 6 listed sites located in only four of the sixteen states of Palau.



This National Park Service list is complete through NPS recent listings posted December 22, 2017.[1]
