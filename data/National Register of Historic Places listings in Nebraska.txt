
This is a list of 1,103 properties and districts in Nebraska that are on the National Register of Historic Places. Of these, 20 are National Historic Landmarks. There are listings in 89 of the state's 93 counties.



This National Park Service list is complete through NPS recent listings posted December 22, 2017.[1]
